Soccer - A Competitive Game

Introduction to Soccer

Soccer, also known as football in many parts of the world, is the most popular sport globally. It is played by over 250 million players in more than 200 countries. The game is known for its simple rules, yet immense excitement and strategy. Also it's known because of famous players such as (Ronaldo, Messi, Ronaldhino, Neymar, R9, Roberto Carlos, etc.)

Soccer is a team sport that involves two teams of eleven players each, who aim to score goals by getting a ball into the opponent's net using any part of the body except the hands and arms. Only the GoalKeeper can use his hands.

Key Facts About Soccer

  • Origins: Soccer's origins can be traced back to ancient cultures, but the modern version was formalized in England in the mid-19th century.
  • World Cup: The FIFA World Cup, held every four years, is the most prestigious tournament in the sport, with over 200 countries participating in qualifiers.
